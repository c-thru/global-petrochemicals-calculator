7. End-of-life processes#

At the end-of-life of the end-use sector products, some of the polymers can be recovered. The processes that split end-of-life products into end-of-life polymers are generated from the product model, and generated recipes are included in Products.

7.1. End-of-life polymer streams#

The following objects are defined to represent polymer streams recovered from end-of-life processes:
